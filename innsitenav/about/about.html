About Narrative Nonsense


After the 2016 election I got possessed with exploring alternate sites on the web to find out what is really going on. At some point, I started to think about joining in public discourse on the events of the day and the historical context. This is how I got interested in political commentary and web design to build a presence of my own for discourse and ideas on the web. I chose narrative_nonsense for a site title after brainstorming a slew of more serious names and realizing I wanted to somehow throw humor into an otherwise not so humorous subject and not take myself or my website too seriously. narrative_nonsense actually underscores a lot of nonsese put out in the government and media narratives today. I move from being agitated by world events and the false narratives given, to being quiet and peaceful in spite of it, simply satisfied to feel that I am getting better at noticing the powers of influence and spin at play.

I have a crazy idea that if more people become more educated in critical thinking, we might hold off the weilders of power from gaining at the average person's expense. I understand the majority who stay away from news given how often news is intentionally presented in confusing ways, or is ignored, or is chaotic. But sometimes I get frustrated with the same people for not fighting back. It seems like so many of the ways people get off track is by following a leader, a party, a government or an "expert" or by subscribing to the 'acepted' narrative religiously, without questioning it.

This site is my first step at playing with technology to bring insight, critical analysis, heart and humor into a fun presentation that will eventually employ a lot more clicking to allow people to snap their way into fun discovery of otherwise heady, difficult material that too often is about the failings of the human spirit as in planners who set up wars and tragedies and regular people who go to the front lines and carry them out. Most people want a world that works for everyone, not just an elite few, and most people care. My goal is to build a discourse that will somehow cultivate people's capacity to employ human spirit with wisdom. I hope you find this site meaningful and enjoyable. William Spencer March 23, 2019
